Pasta-Straight from Italy

Description

Did you ever wonder how a Pasta straight from Napoli tastes like? join us with this recpie and find out!

Alessandro Gatto, our special Italian chef will guide you through the steps.

ingridents

  • All-puprose flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. First, make a nest with flour on a clean work surface
  2. Now, use your hands to gently mix in the flour.
  3. Then, knead! make it a sphere like
  4. From now on, continue the recipe with our Chef all together.

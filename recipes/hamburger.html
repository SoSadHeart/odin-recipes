The Best Burger

Description

Our special home made burgers that you will enjoy.

Prepartion time is going to be 15 minutes. Cook time is about 5 minutes. Enough for 4 servings.

Make sure to buy your favorite pickles, which are the secret ingridents for our burger.

Ingredients

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened, or as needed.
  • 1 pound ground chuck beef(80% lean)
  • 4 6 inch squares parchment paper
  • Salt to taste
  • burger toppings of choice

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oild the grate.
  2. Spread butter on the inside of the buns and toast.
  3. Form meat into 8 loosely-packed balls
  4. Grill for about 45 seconds and turn the sides.
  5. Watch some tutorial on youtube to continue.

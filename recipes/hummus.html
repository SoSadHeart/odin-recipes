Hummus - Your best bud

Description

The best middle eastren taste that you could get in your kitchem.

This recipe will take you hand by hand, until you could get your perfect Hummus.

ingridents

  • Cooked Chickpeas
  • Tahini
  • Extra-Virgin olive oil
  • Fresh lemon Juice
  • Garlic
  • Water
  • Sea Salt

Steps

  1. Once you assemble your ingridients, get out your blender and mix all of the ingridients inside
  2. Add 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin to add extra layer of flavor.
  3. Add roasted redd peppers
  4. Blend in pesto
  5. Enjoy one of the best middle eastren treats inside your kitchen.
